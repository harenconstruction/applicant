APPLY NOW

Superintendent for water and wastewater plant and pump station construction

The successful candidates will reside and work in the Southeastern United States and report to one of several project managers. Viable candidates must have proven Superintendent skills in Water/Wastewater & Pump Station Construction, successfully leading field work in structural concrete, mechanical work, process piping and excavation, piping, and facilities startup. We are looking for seasoned Superintendents with confirmed 10+ years minimum of supervision experience to join the firm and construct projects in the Southeastern United States. Successful candidates will be an integral part of the company’s environmental construction team and oversee projects ($2,000,000 to $20,000,000). Candidates must be strong leaders who can build medium size treatment plants from beginning to end and can provide a history of successful projects.

JOB REQUIREMENTS:

Qualifications

  • 10+ years of successful confirmable supervision experience in managing and constructing water/wastewater treatment plants and pump stations is required.
  • Limited computer skills in MS Office and email communication are a plus.
  • Successful candidates must have experience managing and constructing projects $2 to $20 million while self-performing structural concrete, yard piping, mechanical equipment installation, mechanical and process piping, excavation, and startup experience in a safe and professional manner.
  • Proven track record of leadership skills and strong teamwork oriented success in environmental construction.
  • Successful candidates must be willing to travel the Southeastern United States.
  • Positive attitude and professional customer service skills are a must.
  • Candidates must be detail oriented and have a successful safety record on projects. HCCI prides itself on quality construction and this is a must.
  • Successful candidates must be able to evaluate, hire and motivate field employees to complete projects in an economical, timely, safe and successful manner.
  • Driver’s license is required.

Compensation

  • Annual salary based on past track record of successful projects. Haren offers competitive compensation based on past experience and success.
  • Company truck including truck expenses
  • Personal Medical Benefits at a minor expense
  • 1 week paid vacation after six months of employment
  • 3 weeks paid vacation per year after five years of employment and typical holidays and 5 sick/personal days after 90 days of employment
  • Company 401K contribution, 25% match up to 6% of base salary after 90 days of employment (at the beginning of the next quarter)
  • Out of town living expenses and moving expenses if necessary
